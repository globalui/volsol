Volunteer Abroad Programs

  1. Home
  2. Volunteer Abroad Programs

Find the best and most affordable volunteer abroad programs and make a difference with Volunteering Solutions, one of the best volunteer travel organizations, offering international internships and affordable volunteer opportunities in over 40 destinations across Asia, Africa, Europe, South America, and Australia, throughout the year.

Volunteering & Internship programs are available for a duration of 1 week to 24 weeks, and participants can choose what suits them best. We offer a wide range of programs, including Teaching English, Childcare Projects, Wildlife Conservation, Women Empowerment, Medical Volunteering, Community Development, Short Term Volunteering, Senior Volunteering, Mission Trips and more, for people of all age groups. Pre-med, Medical, Nursing or Dental students can boost their professional skills by joining Medical & Dental Internships.

No matter whether you are a High School student looking for a Spring Break Program or an 'Under 18 Volunteering opportunity', a college student or a gap year traveler seeking a month-long volunteer program overseas, or probably seeking an offbeat family volunteering trip - we have it all for you, so that you can take a step of being a responsible traveler & contribute towards the developing societies.

  • Quick Facts
  • Start Dates : All Year Round
  • Duration : 1-24 Weeks
  • Program Locations : Delhi & Dharamsala - Palampur
  • Minimum Age: 17 years+
  • Certificate - Offered after Program completion
