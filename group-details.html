Volunteer Abroad Programs For University & College Groups

  1. Home
  2. Group Volunteering
  3. University Group Programs

Volunteering Solutions facilitates professional, rewarding and impactful volunteering projects for University Students who are looking for international work exposure, hands-on work, and academic enhancement. The projects offered can be undertaken by Student Groups, University Clubs and Faculty-Led Student Groups. The volunteering opportunities facilitated encompass a wide variety of projects that include Community Development, Education of the underprivileged, Women Empowerment, Medical Volunteering expanding to Physiotherapy, Dental and Nursing Projects. Customized to suit academic, research, and professional learning requirements, the projects offered are apt for students of Social Work, International Relations, Development Studies, Business Management, and Gender Studies besides Medical, Pre-Medical, Public Health, Nursing, Dental and Physiotherapy students. 

Guided under professionals at all times, ‘hands-on’ work is allowed based on competence, skill set and willingness to learn across the entire spectrum. The destinations where opportunities are offered spread across the globe and include several countries in Asia, Africa, Latin America, and Europe. 

The VolSol advantage includes comprehensive information booklets on both the destination and project, ample learning and immersive international travel experiences, seamless in-country transportation and assistance, quality, and safe in-country accommodation and meals.  Please note that each program is tweaked, customized and optimized to meet group needs to ensure a fulfilling experience for the students and also suit the home country needs. 

Faculty-Led Group Volunteering 
Ideal for university students enrolled in a professional program, the Faculty-led projects facilitate practical learning under professional guidance. Medical Students, Dentistry Students, Nursing Students, and Physiotherapy students can opt for medical, dentistry, nursing, and physiotherapy volunteering projects available with us in different destinations. Furthermore, students of Social Work, International Relations, Development Studies, Business Management, and Gender Studies can opt for community development, women empowerment, and girls’ education projects. Groups looking for specific projects for their research work can also reach out for opportunities. 

Student-Led Group Volunteering 
University students looking for an impactful and professional volunteering project during their Summer/Spring break. Students can choose from a wide array of projects across 24 countries. They can take part in teaching kindergarten kids as well as contribute to childcare projects and orphanages. Participants looking to work on the field can head for community development, construction, and renovation projects as well. Medical or pre-med student groups can work in medical clinics and hospitals under the guidance of professionals. The minimum number of students need to be 5 to facilitate a group project.

India

Youth Travel and Volunteer Expedition
Program Overview 

This program offers a balanced combination of trekking, leisure and volunteering activities in the southern state of Kerala which the students will enjoy and cherish for a lifetime. Experience the phenomenal nature through adventure; make a contribution to the local community and carry away huge learning's worth a lifetime.

Highlights
  • Cultural Insight of God’s Own country – Kerala
  • Trekking through Hill stations and Global Bio Diversity Hot Spots
  • Participating in community based projects in rural Kerala
  • Leisure phase with lot of adventure activities
  • Great opportunity for self learning

 

Itinerary in Brief

Day 1 – Group arrival in Kochi, Kerala

Day 2 – Transfer from Kochi to Base Camp (approx-5-6hrs)

Day 3 – Trek to Anaerangal Lakeside

Day 4 – Mathekettan Shola Trek

Day 5 – Chaturangapara Trek

Day 6 – Transfer from Chaturangapara to Haripad ( 5 Hours)

Day 7 to 11 - Project Work At Haripad – Renovation work at a local school

Day 12 to 15  - Transfer to Alleppey for R&R

Day 16 – Day 16: Transfer to Airport

Program Length

The program length is 16 days

Group Size

The minimum group size for the expedition is 10 members.
